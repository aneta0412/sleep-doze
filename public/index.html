How the magic link works

1

Open Doze

The first time you open it, Doze creates your own private link — a web address with a long, random code only you have. No email, no password, nothing to fill in.

2

Share it with the other parent

Tap Copy link in the app and text it to your partner. Whoever opens that link lands on the same tracker — press the button on either phone and it shows up on both.

3

It remembers you

Each phone keeps the link, so your home-screen icon always reopens your family's data. Bookmark it too, just in case. Keep the link in the family — anyone who has it can see and edit.
